Privacy & Listing Standards
We follow a structured review approach before publishing any listing. The platform does not store unnecessary personal data, and all interactions are handled directly between users and profile managers.

Our priority is to maintain a clean, controlled, and privacy-focused browsing environment where users can explore listings without exposure to unwanted risks.

How Listings Are Organized
Each listing is categorized to help users quickly find relevant profiles based on their requirements. Instead of repeating identical information, profiles are structured based on:

Availability schedule
Preferred meeting locations
Communication method (call or messaging)
Response time expectations
How Our Listing System Works
Our platform operates on a direct listing model. Each profile is independently maintained, which allows users to connect without intermediaries. This structure ensures faster responses and a more transparent interaction process.

Profiles are managed individually
Direct communication system
No centralized handling delays
Location-based filtering for convenience
